# Music Streaming Service MVC

Welcome to this musical streaming service experience!
We offer all levels of detail to meet your reverberative needs!


## Artists
Artists are our speciality, they come in many forms and offer basic views along with viewing songs and playlists by those featured!

## Albums
When it comes to albums, we got what you want!
We have: Album covers, song list, editing, deleting, and detailed looks!

## Songs
Songs make it all come together, you want music and we got it.
Get creative and do what you want here and music your heart out!

## Playlists
You like organizing music? Who doesn't!
Create, view various playlists by likes, and add songs of course!
